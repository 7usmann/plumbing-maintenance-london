## Brand
No confirmed brand colours found. Given the multi-trade, established-firm
positioning (shopfront, Ltd company, family-run feel), suggest a grounded, trustworthy
palette — deep green or navy as primary with a warm neutral accent — steering away
from anything that reads as a single-trade colour cliché (e.g. plumbing blue) since
this business covers more ground than one trade.

## What NOT to invent
- No Gas Safe or other certification claims — see placeholder above.
- Don't present the 9.4 rating as fact without knowing its source.
